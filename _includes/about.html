ABOUT ME

As a full stack software engineer, I thrive on the challenges I am met with in my work. These challenges spur a constant reevaluation of my thought process and approach to solving problems. It is fascinating to see the various ways one problem can be solved and the creativity and elegance in each solution. Instead of viewing challenges as a deterrent, I see them as a way to grow and foster my skills.


I have experience creating full-stack Javascript applications, both on web and mobile, utilizing technologies such as the MEAN stack, React Native, and MySQL amongst others. I find it extremely gratifying and rewarding to be able to implement a seed of an idea into something that can solve a real world problem or be of use to someone else.
